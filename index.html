BTS

BTS is a k-pop group consisting of 7 members. Coming from an entertainment company that was hardly known in the Korean entertainment industry, BTS have grown to become the first k-pop group to break into the western music market. While the group is known for their show-stopping performances, their songs are just as memorable. Ranging from pop to rap to RnB, anyone can find a song to enjoy. Also notable is the message behind their music. Their songs touch on subjects like the outdated education system, depression, self-empowerment, and more. One common message BTS has stated time and again is that they want to be a voice for the younger generations. At a 2018 United Nations conference, group leader Kim Namjoon gave a speech where he urged people to love themselves and speak themselves.
